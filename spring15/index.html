hello there! i want to introduce an extension of the playlists i've been making. as i hone my design skills and learn how to code, i'll be creating a website centered around a theme every season. it'll be more than just music: shopping, inspiration, and many other topics will encompass a "lifestyle" of sorts exploring the theme. i'm really excited to share a deeply personal and creative outlet with all of you!

for spring, the theme is exercise. with the "athleisure" trend reaching mainstream, i wanted to define my interpretation of what it means to look good while feeling good. recently there was an article written on style.com that explains the physical body being the new status symbol of luxury. abs replace furs. soulcycle replaces maxfields. cold presses replace bottle service. by no means am i above this trend, and i can tell you first-hand how ridiculous it is to pay forty dollars for riding a stationary bike for an hour. but besides the bill, working out has become a way to keep a routine since i started regularly going to the gym a little more than a year ago. sometimes it feels like the only part of my day when i can catch up on podcasts and not check my emails.

anyway, i hope you like my first installment of many series to come. please feel free to leave me feedback!
